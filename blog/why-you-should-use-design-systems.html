Why you should use design systems

Design systems are essential for companies and teams to create consistent and cohesive designs across all their products and services. In this blog article, we'll explore what a design system is, why it's important, and how to create one that works for your organization.

by Iulian Pistol

Design systems have become increasingly popular in the design industry, and for good reason. There are many benefits to implementing a design system in your company, which can greatly impact the quality and efficiency of your design work. In this article, we'll explore some of the key benefits of having a design system in place.

snippet from the Universal Design System

Systems scale

One of the biggest benefits of a design system is its scalability. With a design system in place, you can easily scale your designs to meet the needs of your growing company. This is especially important as you expand into new markets or add new products and services to your portfolio. A design system ensures that your designs are consistent, no matter how large your company becomes.

snippet from the Universal Design System

Systems are uniform and consistent

A design system creates uniformity in your designs, which is essential for building a strong and recognizable brand. By using a standardized set of design elements and guidelines, you can ensure that your designs look and feel consistent, no matter who is working on them. This helps to create a cohesive look and feel for your company, which is crucial for building a strong brand identity.

snippet from the design system Untitled UI

Design systems shorten the design process

Another significant benefit of a design system is that it saves time. With a design system in place, designers can quickly and easily access the design elements they need to complete a project. This not only saves time, but also ensures that the designs are consistent and of high quality.

Promotes the company's philosophy and brand: A design system helps to promote your company's philosophy and brand by establishing a consistent look and feel for all of your designs. This is especially important for companies that have a strong brand philosophy, as it helps to communicate that philosophy to your audience through your designs.

snippet from the Universal Design System

Design systems improve collaboration

Finally, a design system helps to improve collaboration between colleagues. By using a standardized set of design elements and guidelines, designers can work together more effectively and efficiently. This helps to ensure that everyone is on the same page and working towards the same goal, which is essential for achieving high-quality designs.

snippet from the Universal Design System

Key takeaway

A design system is a valuable tool for any company, big or small. By creating uniformity in your designs, saving time, promoting your company's philosophy and brand, and improving collaboration between colleagues, a design system can greatly impact the quality and efficiency of your design work. Whether you're a small startup or a large corporation, implementing a design system is a step in the right direction for your company's design work.
